About Us

Join A Club platform allows students to access information about any club at their respective school, at any time. It facilitates students' involvement, as it encourages participation by avoiding the hectic clutter of "join a club" day. Sponsors may eventually have access to these clubs, to allow discoveries and new relations to form. Our own experience with activities day at University inspired us to improve this important part of integration and involvement.
